Auto Generating Tool for Making Minute Of Installation

Oct 2017-Mar 2018

The purpose of this app is to make a progress process more efficient. FYI, a progress process is a process to collect a fee, based on what we material that has been installed in a project. This process needs many requirements, one of them is minutes of installation (Berita Acara Pemasangan) . So, in conventional way, the process is like this:
1.Supervisor should take a picture for the material that has been installed ,
2.Supervisor give the picture to a project admin,
3.Project Admin should type all the information to the template such as time when the picture was taken, floor level,etc and choose the picture to insert in minutes of installation.

With this apps, a supervisor directly just input all the information needed and take the picture via Appsheet that connect to Google Spreadsheet in his smartphone then with the help of Google Script, spreadsheet will automatically generate all the information and picture to become a ready to print minutes of installation.
